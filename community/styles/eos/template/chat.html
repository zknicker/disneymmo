Hello and welcome to DisneyMMO's live chat room! The rest of the community is great, but this is the quickest and most efficient way to talk with the rest of the DisneyMMO population.

We just need you to follow a couple of really basic guidelines. Also avoid using light font colors, and using otherwise obnoxious font styling. And finally, remember to respect the decisions and requests of the moderation staff present to preserve the family friendly atmosphere DisneyMMO.com strives to maintain. They're enjoying the site in the same way you are, and only want to keep the community an enjoyable place for everyone.

Have a great time!
